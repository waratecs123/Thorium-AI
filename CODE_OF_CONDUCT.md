## Golden Rule
**Be respectful.** We're here to learn and help others learn about AI.

### Do:
- Help beginners
- Politely correct mistakes
- Suggest improvements
- Translate content
- Ask questions

### Don't:
- Insult or belittle others
- Use sexist/racist language
- Spam or self-promote
- Share personal information
- Be aggressive

### If Something Goes Wrong
1. **Stop** the interaction
2. **Report** to maintainers
3. **We'll handle it** quickly and privately

Violators may receive a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban.

**Goal:** Create a safe, welcoming space for everyone interested in AI.
