About This Project

Thorium-AI is an educational project created by a single enthusiast. The goal is to make AI knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of background.

This project is completely free — no subscriptions, no payments, no hidden fees. All content is available to anyone with internet access.

As one person, I invest time and resources into creating this encyclopedia because I believe AI knowledge should not be locked behind paywalls or available only to students of elite universities.

Every article is written and created manually. No AI-generated content — only verified information explained clearly.

Content Structure

The encyclopedia is organized into 8 sections:

  • Practical AI – Everyday applications
  • AI Fundamentals – History and philosophy
  • Mathematics for AI – Essential math
  • Machine Learning – Core algorithms
  • Neural Networks – Biological to artificial
  • Deep Learning – Advanced architectures
  • Applied Directions – NLP, Computer Vision, RL
  • Advanced Topics – Ethics, limitations, future

Each topic begins with an introduction for beginners and progresses to advanced concepts.
